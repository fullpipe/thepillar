Alexandra Fiodorova is a procedural artist and art director whose work explores the interplay between generative systems, cause and effect, and the abstract in figurative forms.

She uses 3D to build systems that generate form and motion, driven by a fascination with rules, pointlessness, and counterform.

She loves board games, raves, numbers (4), and puzzles. Her last known favorite color is blue.

Her favorite project is The Pillar (you can listen to it).

This year her works were featured in two design festivals: DEMO festival and Kinomural.
